Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

🔒 Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when using our services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account
  • Order Information: Project details, company name, domain preferences, and budget when placing an order
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through Stripe — we never store your card details on our servers
  • Communication Data: Messages sent through our AI Support Hub or contact forms

📊 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our web development services
  • Process orders, payments, and deliver your website projects
  • Send order status updates and project-related communications
  • Respond to your support inquiries and provide technical assistance
  • Protect against fraud and ensure the security of our platform

🛡️ Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: All data is transmitted over HTTPS with TLS encryption
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage
  • Authentication: JWT-based secure session management
  • Payment Security: PCI-DSS compliant payment processing via Stripe

🤝 Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your information only in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: Payment processors (Stripe) and hosting providers necessary to deliver our services
  • Legal Compliance: When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

🍪 Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser storage technologies to enhance your experience:

  • Authentication Tokens: Stored in localStorage to maintain your login session
  • Language Preferences: Your selected language (EN/AR) is remembered across visits
  • Cart Data: Your product selections are temporarily stored for convenience

We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising trackers.

⚙️ Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

👶 Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

🔄 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. Your continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

📬 Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your data rights, please contact us through our Support Hub. We aim to respond to all privacy inquiries within 48 hours.
